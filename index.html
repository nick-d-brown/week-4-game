Crystal Collector!

Game Rules and description:

At the start of the game a random number will be chosen. The goal is to get your score to equal the random number that was chosen. There are 4 crystals in the cave, and each crystal is worth a random value. Click on the crystals in order to increase your score. If your score is equal to the random word chosen you win. If your score is greater than the number chosen you lose. You are allowed an unlimited number of clicks on the crystals so long as your score does not exceed the value of the random number chosen. At the start of every new game a new random number will be chosen and the values of each crystal will be changed.

Good Luck!
